# Affiliate Review Engine Skill

## Name
Affiliate Review Engine

## Description
Use this skill for ClickBank, affiliate product reviews, Pinterest promotion, Facebook community posts, comparison posts, landing page copy and ethical affiliate monetisation.

## Trigger when

- The user mentions ClickBank, affiliate reviews, product reviews, Pinterest, Facebook promotion, funnels, email swipes or product evaluation.
- The user wants to adapt a proven review format to another product.

## Review structure

1. Executive summary.
2. Product overview.
3. Who it is for.
4. Main promise.
5. How it works.
6. Key features.
7. Pros.
8. Cons.
9. Pricing/offer notes.
10. Bonuses or funnel notes.
11. Realistic verdict.
12. CTA.

## Tone

- Clear, direct and persuasive.
- Helpful rather than hype-heavy.
- No fake personal results.
- No invented testimonials.
- Disclose affiliate relationship where appropriate.

## Pinterest content format

For each pin:

- Pin title.
- Pin description.
- Overlay text.
- Image prompt.
- CTA.
- Keywords.

## Facebook community format

- Short value-led hook.
- Relatable problem.
- Practical insight.
- Soft bridge to product.
- Disclosure-aware CTA.

## Compliance rules

- Do not make medical, financial or guaranteed income claims.
- Do not invent product details.
- Verify current product page, pricing, refund terms and claims before finalising copy.
- Use balanced pros and cons.
