# Social Content Engine Skill

## Name
Social Content Engine

## Description
Use this skill for finished, ready-to-post Deus Intus social content across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Facebook, Threads, Pinterest and X.

## Trigger when

- The task asks for captions, posts, carousels, reels, hooks, hashtags, content calendars or community posts.
- The user asks for Deus Intus daily content.
- The output needs to be ready to paste into Canva, Buffer, Postiz, Make.com, Google Sheets or a scheduler.

## Default output mode

Finished content, not outlines.

## Brand tone

- Natural, uplifting, calm and strong.
- Spiritual but grounded.
- Inner power, discipline, self-mastery, awareness, healing and sovereignty.
- Premium, short, memorable lines.

## Required content structure

For image posts:

1. On-image text.
2. Caption.
3. CTA.
4. Hashtags.
5. Alt text.
6. Canva/image prompt if needed.

For carousels:

1. Slide-by-slide copy.
2. Visual direction per slide.
3. Caption.
4. CTA.
5. Hashtags.

For reels/shorts:

1. Hook.
2. Voiceover script.
3. Scene list.
4. On-screen text.
5. Caption.
6. CTA.

## Hook styles

Use hooks such as:

- “You are not behind. You are being refined.”
- “Your energy is not random. It is your instruction.”
- “The life you want requires a state you have not mastered yet.”
- “Discipline is not punishment. It is devotion to your future self.”

## Hashtag rule

Use a balanced hashtag stack:

- 3 brand hashtags
- 5 niche spiritual/self-mastery hashtags
- 5 growth/wellbeing hashtags
- 2 platform/discovery hashtags

## Avoid

- fake urgency
- overused hustle quotes
- excessive emojis
- long rambling captions
- medical or mental health claims
